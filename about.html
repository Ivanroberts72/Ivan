About TR.Ivan

Hey there! I'm Womulira Ivan, but my learners call me TR.Ivan.

I teach Mathematics and ICT at ST. MARY ASSUMPTA SECONDARY SCHOOL. With over 5 years of teaching experience, I’ve made it my mission to break down tough concepts into fun, easy-to-digest lessons.

This platform, Learn With TR.Ivan, is a digital extension of my classroom — built for learners, teachers, and parents looking to improve performance and enjoy the learning process.

Let’s raise the bar for rural education — and make sure no child is left behind.
